About Me

Md. Ashrafuzzaman
Assistant Professor, Department of Education
Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University, Bangladesh (BDU)
Email: ashraf@edu.bdu.ac.bd

Md. Ashrafuzzaman is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Education at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University, Bangladesh (BDU). Concurrently, he is also performing as a researcher and teacher trainer in several renowned organizations. Previously, he worked as a Research Coordinator in the EIA-DU-OU (UK) Research Collaboration Programme at the Institute of Education and Research, University of Dhaka. He has gained eleven years of experience in the field of teaching and educational research with different organizations. In 2014, he received an MPhil degree entitled ‘Impact of In-Service Training (Cluster Meeting) on Primary Teachers Classroom Practice’ in English Language Education from the Institute of Education and Research (IER), University of Dhaka (DU). He has also completed his BEd (Hons.) and MEd from IER, DU. He has researched significant areas such as teachers’ training programs, underprivileged children’s education (sex workers, transgender, and slum children), English language education, classroom practice, teaching methods and techniques, assessment and feedback practice, and ICT in education. Moreover, he has published 23 research articles, reports, and a book chapter on renowned national and international peer-reviewed journals. Recently he has also published a book titled ‘Ensuring Education for All: Inclusive Education’ in Bangla. Nowadays, he is highly motivated to prepare himself for enrolling PhD program in Technology Education that may be uplifting the professional purpose.

BDU at a glance

Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Digital University, Bangladesh-Bangladesh's first specialized Government digital university. On 26 July 2016, the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh enacted a law in the National Assembly to establish this university on a 50-acre site adjacent to Bangabandhu Hi-Tech Park in Kaliakair, Gazipur District. On June 12, 2018, Professor of Civil Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering, former Vice-Chancellor of the National University and former Vice-Chancellor of the Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Professor Dr. Munaz Ahmed Noor was appointed as the first Vice Chancellor of the University by His Excellency the President of the People's Republic of Bangladesh and the Chancellor of the Universities of Bangladesh Md. Abdul Hamid. To make Digital Bangladesh sustainable by implementing the 'Vision 2021' of the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh, in line with the Fourth Industrial Revolution, to educate, enhance research and improve digital knowledge in various fields of information and communication technology at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels to turn young generation into skilled human resources and entrepreneurs Establishment of the University.
